What is asterisk?

asterisk is a platform designed to help people track the progress of they're life. Everyone is unique and has different was of accomplishing goals. asterisk is designed to be as minimal as possible so that it can be incorperated into life very easily. It should give you the data you need but be easy to interact with and update. At the moment, it operates using self-reporting quizzes. Soon, there will be a pool of questions that can be pulled from to gather stronger corrolations. Also, APIs will be used heavily to automatically track things like financials and physical health.
